Built for the Hangar

Engineered for Aviation Professionals

Every detail of Hangar Bags is designed for the unique demands of aircraft maintenance and inspection.

Instant Attachment

Securely mounts to your aircraft's structure in seconds with aviation-safe fastening. No tools required to install the bag itself — just clip and go.

Multi-Pocket Organization

Purpose-built pockets and slots keep wrenches, screwdrivers, flashlights, safety wire, and inspection tools organized and accessible.

Aircraft-Safe Materials

Made from non-abrasive, non-conductive materials that won't scratch paint, damage composites, or interfere with avionics and electrical systems.

Lightweight & Durable

Heavy-duty construction withstands the demands of daily hangar life while remaining light enough to never add meaningful weight to the aircraft.

Reduces FOD Risk

By keeping tools secured to the aircraft, Hangar Bags dramatically reduces the risk of foreign object debris — the #1 preventable safety hazard in aviation maintenance.

Universal Fit

Adjustable mounting system fits single-engine pistons to light twins. Whether you're working on a Cessna 172 or a Beechcraft Baron, Hangar Bags adapts.

Simple by Design

Three Steps. Zero Hassle.

Getting started with Hangar Bags is as easy as a preflight check.

1

Attach

Clip the Hangar Bag onto your aircraft's structure — wing strut, landing gear, engine mount, or cowling edge — using the secure, non-marring fastening system.

2

Load

Fill the organized compartments with the tools and supplies you need for the job. Every pocket is sized for the items aviation mechanics actually use.

3

Work

Get to work with everything at your fingertips. No more climbing down the ladder, walking across the hangar, or hunting through a toolbox on the floor.
